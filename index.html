How it works

Four steps to unlocking your home equity

Understanding the HELOC process before you apply puts you in a stronger position with lenders.

1

Check your equity

Use our free calculator to find out how much equity your home currently holds.

2

Review your credit

Most lenders require a credit score of 620 or higher. A score of 700+ gets the best rates.

3

Compare lenders

Rates, fees, and terms vary widely. Our lender comparison content makes it easy to shop around.

4

Apply & draw

Once approved, draw funds as needed up to your credit limit — only pay interest on what you use.
