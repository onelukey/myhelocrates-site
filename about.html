Our mission

We believe every homeowner deserves clear, honest HELOC information

For many American homeowners, a Home Equity Line of Credit is one of the most powerful financial tools available. It can fund a life-changing renovation, consolidate expensive debt, or provide a safety net in uncertain times. Yet the information needed to make a smart HELOC decision is often buried in jargon, scattered across lender websites, or designed to push you toward a product rather than help you understand one.

We built MyHelocRates.com to fix that. Our goal is simple: give every US homeowner — whether they are exploring HELOCs for the first time or ready to apply tomorrow — the tools, rates data, and plain-English guides they need to make a confident, informed decision. No sign-ups, no sales calls, no agenda.

Transparent

We clearly disclose how we make money (advertising and affiliate relationships) and it never influences our editorial content or calculator tools.

Educational first

Every page is built to inform, not sell. We explain the risks and downsides of HELOCs as clearly as the benefits.

Always current

HELOC rates move with Federal Reserve decisions. We review and update our rate data and content weekly to keep information accurate.
